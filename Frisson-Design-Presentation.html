FRISSON

Find Your Frequency

Design Philosophy

Less interface.
More resonance.

Minimal by intention

No ambient sounds. No guided voices during sessions. No visual clutter. Just pure sine waves and your awareness.

Personal by design

Your brainwave frequencies are unique. Frisson helps you discover the specific combinations that create resonance in your body.

Physical feedback

That tingle you feel when frequencies align? We call it frisson. The app is built around capturing and recreating that moment.

App Structure

Three paths. One purpose.

1

Guided Discovery

A voice-led 25-minute protocol that systematically explores frequency combinations. Tap to mark when you feel frisson.

Best for first-time users

2

Manual Discovery

Free exploration with real-time controls. Adjust carrier frequency and beat frequency. Mark discoveries as you find them.

For experienced explorers

3

Session Mode

Meditate with your discovered frequencies. Choose duration from 1-60 minutes. Pure tones, minimal interface.

Daily practice

Home Screen

Three clear paths forward

The home screen presents three options without hierarchy clutter. The Guided Discovery card is emphasized for new users. Once they have saved frequencies, the Session card becomes more prominent.

Design note

Cards use generous padding and subtle shadows. Touch targets are large. The settings icon is minimal and tucked away—this is not a settings-heavy app.

Manual Discovery Mode

Free exploration

For experienced users who want direct control. Two elegant sliders with haptic feedback at key intervals. The carrier frequency slider (100-400 Hz) controls the base tone. The beat frequency slider (4-8 Hz) controls the binaural difference.

Interaction design

Sliders have physical weight—they decelerate naturally when released. Subtle haptic ticks at each Hz. The central visualization responds in real-time to frequency changes.

Session Mode • Active

Pure meditation

The session screen is radically minimal. A circular progress arc shows time remaining. The central dot pulses gently with the binaural beat. Nothing else. The screen dims after 5 seconds of inactivity but stays on to provide ambient visual.

Screen behavior

Tap anywhere to reveal controls (pause, end early). The arc is the only moving element—it progresses imperceptibly slowly, creating calm rather than urgency.
