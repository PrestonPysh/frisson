You're about to discover something remarkable — your brain's unique resonant frequency.

Through your headphones, we'll play a slightly different tone in each ear. Your brain perceives the difference between these tones as a gentle pulse — this is called a binaural beat.

These beats can guide your brain into specific states. When you sleep, your brain naturally cycles through these frequencies — slow delta waves during deep rest, theta rhythms during REM and dreaming, alpha waves when relaxed but alert.

Frisson helps you find where your brain responds most powerfully — that moment when you feel a tingle down your spine, a wave of calm, or sudden clarity.
